Global Super Store Dataset Analysis

Author: Sahil Wikhe

Project 1: Data Mining

In this project, the Global Super Store dataset is analyzed to answer several business questions:

  • What are the top-performing product categories and regions in terms of sales and profitability?
  • How does discounting affect profitability, and is there an optimal discount rate?
  • What shipping methods are most effective, and do they vary by region?
  • Are there any noticeable trends in sales performance over time (seasonality, yearly growth)?

The project uses Python along with libraries like pandas, numpy, matplotlib, and seaborn to clean the data, perform exploratory analysis, and generate insightful visualizations.

Data Mining Process & Business Questions

1. Business Understanding

Objective: Analyze sales, profits, and operational efficiency to optimize strategies.

  • Identify top-performing product categories and regions.
  • Determine the effect of discounting on profitability.
  • Examine the effectiveness of various shipping methods.
  • Detect trends in sales performance over time.
2. Data Understanding

The dataset contains 51,290 rows and 24 columns. Key columns include Sales, Profit, Discount, Ship Mode, and Category. Missing values in the Postal Code column are dropped as they are not relevant.

3. Data Preparation
  • Drop columns with excessive missing values (e.g., Postal Code).
  • Convert date columns (Order Date and Ship Date) to datetime format.
  • Create derived metrics:
    • Profit Margin = Profit / Sales
    • Discount bins for analysis
4. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
  • Sales and Profit Analysis: Aggregate data by category and region using bar charts and heatmaps.
  • Discount Analysis: Use scatter plots and line charts to explore discount vs. profitability.
  • Shipping Mode Analysis: Compare sales by shipping method and regional variations using boxplots.
  • Time Series Analysis: Plot monthly trends and yearly growth rates to uncover patterns.
5. Evaluation
  • Technology is the top-performing category; the Central region is most profitable.
  • High discounts can lead to negative profit margins, suggesting the need to find an optimal discount range.
  • Standard Class is the most common shipping method, though costs vary by region.
  • Sales display seasonality with significant peaks during certain months.
6. Deployment

The visualizations and insights can be exported for presentations or integrated into dashboards for continuous monitoring.
